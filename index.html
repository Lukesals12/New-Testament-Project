Why I Love The Gospel of Jesus Christ

The gospel of Jesus Christ has changed the trajectory of my life. Throughout my life, I was blessed to learn the princples of the gospel. This helped me make the decision to serve a full time mission. I was called to Mexico City and I loved every moment of it. For two years I devoted all of my time to the gospel of Jesus Christ. I learned how important the gospel is and how it can change lives. I am extrememly glad I took this class becasue I learned more in depth about my Savior. This class reminded me how Jesus Christ wants to be apart of our lives. He is the light and the life of this world. I learned so much more about the Saviors life in this class.
